Hi, I'm a PhD student in BIRD Lab. I earned dual bachelor's degrees in Computer Science and Automation from Zhejiang University followed by a Master's degree in Robotics from the University of Bristol. Then I worked for two years as a prediction algorithm engineer at Horizon Robotics (a leading autonomous driving/robotics company in China). With a strong interest in intelligent robotics, my current research focuses on the hardware design and intelligent control of bio-inspired humanoid robots.
