Xu Zhu is a PhD student. He earned dual bachelor's degrees in Computer Science and Automation from Zhejiang University followed by a Master's degree in Robotics from the University of Bristol. He then worked for two years as a prediction algorithm engineer at Horizon Robotics (a leading autonomous driving/robotics company in China). With a strong interest in intelligent robotics, his current research focuses on the hardware design and intelligent control of bio-inspired humanoid robots.
